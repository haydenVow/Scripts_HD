

Procedure

1\. Connect the harvest tube of the STR 200L to the dip tube of the STR 2000L

2\. Pump the entirety of the cell suspension into the STR 2000L

3\. Add the GF and L-glutamine solution  




IGF- 750 mL (M13137)

EGF - 150 mL (M13136)

L-glutamine - 11163 mL (M13142, A12943)

Antifoam C - 75 mL (12931)

  

  




pH probe (offline): 7.53

pH probe (bag): 7.57
