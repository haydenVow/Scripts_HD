1) Growth medium: modified L-15 (Mercurio 2014)

  1. Combined the following reagents and media to make 250mL of medium

  * Leibovitz L-15
  * \- NaCl (20.2 g/L)
  * \- KCl (0.54 g/L) 
  * \- CaCl2 (0.6 g/L)
  * \- Na2SO4 (1g/L)
  * \- MgCl2 (3.9g/L)
  * \- Glucose (1g/L)
  *  ~~\- Taurine (25 mg/L)~~ (Potentially toxic and so excluded from this medium)
  * \- Insulin (10 mg/mL) 
  * \- L-glutamine (100 mg/L)
  * \- Vitamin E (1.75 mg/L)
  * \- 1x antibiotic/antimycotic  solution 

2\. Check the pH of the media and ensure it is pH 7.6.  

3\. Sterile filter solution using a 0.22 um filter unit




2) Growth medium: L-15 + 2% FBS + Vit E + L-Glut + 1X antibiotic/antimycotic
(Bulkagov 2002)

1\. Combine the following reagents to create250mL L-15+ grown medium:

  * \- FBS (2%)
  * \- insulin (50 mg/L)
  * \- Î±-tocopheryl acetate (Vitamin E) (1.75 mg/mL)
  * \- Glutamine (100 mg/L)
  * \- 1x antibiotic/antimycotic 

2\. Check the pH of the media and ensure it is pH 7.6.  

3\. Sterile filter solution using a 0.22 um filter unit




3.1) Artifical seawater (ASW) solution for creating growth medium and for use
during the biopsy and isolation wash steps.  (Cold Spring Harbor Protocols)  
  
1\. Weight out the following reagents and dissolve in MilliQ water to create a
total of 1L of ASW

\- NaCl (26.29 g/L or 450 mM)

\- KCl (0.74 g/L or 10 mM)

\- CaCl2 (0.99 g/L or 9 mM)

\- MgCl2*6H2O (6.09 g/L or 30 mM)

\- MgSO4*7H2O (3.94 g/L or 16 mM)

2\. Ensure the solution has a pH of 7.8  
3\. Sterile filter  using a 0.22 um filter unit.

3.2) Growth medium ASW + 2% FBS + 1X anti-anti  

1\. Combine the following reagents to create 250mL  ASW + 2% FBS + 1X
antibiotic/antimycotic  grown medium:

  * FBS (2%)
  * 1X antibiotic/antimycotic 

2\. Sterile filter solution using a 0.22 um filter unit.

3.3) Collagenase dissolved in ASW (0.5 mg/mL)

1\. Combine collagenase with CMF-SW to achieve 0.5 mg/mL concentration.
