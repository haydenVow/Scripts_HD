

Process:

Prepare 150 mL of each of the following media conditions:

  1. Freestyle 293 + other*
  2. Hycell CHO + other*
  3. RPMI + other*
  4. DMEM/F12+ other*  
  
Other - Difco yeast extract (1g/L) + L-Ornithine monohydrochloride (20 mg/L) +
Putrescine dihydrochloride (20 mg/L) + IGF-1 LONG R3 (50 μg/L) + EGF (10 μg/L)
+ Anti-clumping agent (1:100)  
  
*only add growth factor on the day the growth media is required
