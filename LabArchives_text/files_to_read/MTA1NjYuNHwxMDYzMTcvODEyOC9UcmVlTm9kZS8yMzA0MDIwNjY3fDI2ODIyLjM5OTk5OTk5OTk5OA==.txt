Process


  1. Aliquoted 20 mL of RPMI Nuvonis medium with glucose and 20 mL RPMI Nuvonis medium without glucose 
    * Media is in fridge 129 in main lab
  2. Removed flasks from incubator 
  3. Took 1 x 550 uL samples from each E-flask
  4. Performed cell counts and recorded in table below
  5. Transferred cultures to 2 x 50 mL tubes
  6. Centrifuged at 500 x g for 5 min
  7. Aspirated 100% of supernatant
  8. Resuspended each pellet in 20 mL of respective media
  9. Strained cultures through 40 um strainers and tansferred back to E-125s 
  10. Returned cultures to incubate at 37'C, 5% CO2, 120 RPM
