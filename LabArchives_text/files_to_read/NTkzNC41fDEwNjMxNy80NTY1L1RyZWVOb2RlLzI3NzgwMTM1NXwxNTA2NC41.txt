

Protocol:

  1. Added 140uL of Reagent B to 7000uL of Reagent A to make WR solution (~ 7mL)
  2. Created dilutions from 2000 - 6.25 mg/mL of BSA in a 96 well plate
  3. Added 25uL of each BSA dilution to 200uL of WR solution (2 replicates)
  4. Observed a colour change and incubated for 30 mins at 37 degrees.

Incubation time: 14:45 - 15:15
