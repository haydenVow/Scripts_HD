Process:

7268-DOLLY12-Sheep P9-2of3 is in Thermo incubator.

  1. Wash wells twice with 150uL/well of PBS
  2. Fix cells for 15 minutes with 4% PFA (100uL/well) at room temp. 10:29-10:44
  3. Stain cells with DAPI (1:1000 in PBS) by adding 100uL/well and incubating for 5 min. 10:45-10:50
  4. Wash wells twice with 150uL/well of PBS for 5 minutes each.
  5. Image on the Zaber on 5x (one image per well).
  6. Parafilm plate and store in fridge (4 degrees C).
