

Made up 200mM stock soltuion (29.882g/L)

  1. Weigh out powder for desired volume into a vessel
  2. Fill the vessel with MilliQ water
  3. Shake/mix vessel to dissolve powder
  4. Sterile Filter solution in the BSC
  5. Store in fridge or freezer

For this stock I made up 3L (87g) in a 5L Schott bottle and filtered this
using cup filters in the BSC.
