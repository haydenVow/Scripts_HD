

Corning PRF DMEM/F12 powdered media (without PR, L-glut, & Sodium bicarb).
Stock ID: 3793

\- Add 365 mg/L L-glut or 12.5mL/L of 200mM L-glut solution

\- Add 2.438 g/L NaHCO3

\- recommended working pH is 7-7.4. Aim for 7.1 pH as filter sterilising can
raise pH by 0.1-0.3.

  

To make 10L of Corning media:

  * 1x bottle of 10L corning powder
  * 125mL 200mM L-Glut
  * 24.38g Sodium Bicarb
  * 9.875L MilliQ

pH test pre-filtration: 7.2

pH test post-filtration: 7.2
