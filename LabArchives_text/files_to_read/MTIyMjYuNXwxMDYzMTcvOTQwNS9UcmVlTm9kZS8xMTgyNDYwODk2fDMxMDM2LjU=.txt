Aim/Purpose
  
  
Background

The addition of Fatty acids (FA) to fibroblast culture media could increase
proliferation, cellular fat content, collagen synthesis and collagen lattice
contraction, but the effects are highly dependent on cell type, FA type and FA
concentration.

Literature Review and Background can be found here.

Aim

To identify whether the addition of lipids to the Nuvonis cultures can improve
the fat content and thus mouthfeel of our product.

Hypothesis

The addition of lipids will add a texture reminiscent of fat
