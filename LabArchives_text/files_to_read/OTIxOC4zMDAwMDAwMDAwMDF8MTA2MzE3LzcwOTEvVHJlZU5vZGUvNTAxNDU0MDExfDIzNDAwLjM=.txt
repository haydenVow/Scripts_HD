Process
  
  
  1. Took 2 x 50 uL aliquots from E-125 culture and performed 3 x cell counts. Average cell count was 35,173,333
  2. 35,173,333 cells per 20 mL = 1,684,000 cells per mL
  3. Transferred 20 mL culture to 1 x 50 mL tube
  4. Centrifuged at 500 x g for 5 minutes
  5. Aspirated 100% of supernatant
  6. Re-suspended in 6 mL of sterile dPBS (want 500,000 cells per mL to add 20 uL containing 10,000 cells per well = 50,000 cells per mL in culture)
  7. Dispensed cell suspension into 1 x well of 12-well reservoir in the BSC
  8. Ran cell seeding protocol on OT2 
    1. Start time:
    2. Run time:
  9. Moved plates to incubator at 37'C, 5%CO2 (incubator #119, main lab)
