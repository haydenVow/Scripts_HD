

Procedure




Glucose strip test:

1\. Collect cell sample in a container (i.e., 50 mL falcon tube)

2\. Remove one reagent strip from the bottle and immediately replace cap on
bottle.

3\. Dip the test area of the strip completely into the cell sample and remove
immediately drawing edge of the strip against the rim of the container to
remove excess cell suspension.

4\. Start the timer for 30 seconds

5\. Compare the glucose test are to the glucose colour chart exactly 30
seconds after wetting

Note: Ignore any colour changes that may occur after 30 seconds

6\. Record test test below

  

Results:

~1 g/L (1/10%)

5.5 mmol/L

100 mg/dL
