Process
  
  
  1. Took 2 x 50 uL samples from each flask (HyCell/RPMI and Freestyle)
  2. Performed 4 x cell counts
    1. Freestyle average: 30,840,000
    2. RPMI average: 30,080,000
  3. Added 20 mL of each media (fresh with growth factors) to 2 x new E-125 flasks
  4. Added 2 mL containing ~3 million cells (~150K per mL) of each culture to their new flask
  5. Replaced in incubator (RPM 150, 37'C, 5%CO2)
