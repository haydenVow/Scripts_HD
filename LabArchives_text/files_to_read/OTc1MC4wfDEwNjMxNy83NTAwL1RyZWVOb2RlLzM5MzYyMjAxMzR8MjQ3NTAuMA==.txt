

Frozen cell pellets extracted from pooled adherent cultures were taken to UNSW
on ice.

RNA extraction was performed as per protocol

RNA elution's were placed in the freezer at UNSW, box 7-3-1. Tubes were
labelled with the date, the gene knockout from which they were taken, the time
since nucleofection, and the round of nucleofection (eg 23/3/22, LARS, Day 5,
round 7).
