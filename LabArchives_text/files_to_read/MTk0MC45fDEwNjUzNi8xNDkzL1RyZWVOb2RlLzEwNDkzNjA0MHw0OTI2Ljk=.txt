

Procedure




Resuspended the cells in 100% fresh GM

1\. Prepared 200 mL of 3:1 (RPMI:Hycell/CDM4) media with the required volume
of GF and place in water bath at 37'C

\- Media ID: M12708

\- IGF ID: A12593

\- EGF ID: A12215

2\. Transfer the cell suspension (from 12181) into a 200 mL centrifuge tube
and centrifuge at 310x g for 10 mins

3\. Aspirate the supernatant and resuspend the pellet in the fresh GM.

4\. Return to shaking incubator at 120 RPM.

Culture ID: 12372
