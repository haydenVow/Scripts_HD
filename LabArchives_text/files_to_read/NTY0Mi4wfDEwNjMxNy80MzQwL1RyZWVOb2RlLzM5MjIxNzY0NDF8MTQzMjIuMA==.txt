### Observations/Summary:

Opentron observations/issues:

Zaber observations/issues:  

1) Observed clumpy objects in wells on DAPI channel - did not look exactly
like clumped cells and did not light up on 488 channel. Avani observed the
same phenomenon while imaging her suspension samples, for which she used the
same staining solution. Loaded 2 x 100 uL samples of plain dPBS, dPBS + sytox
green, dPBS + Hoechst, and the staining solution used to determine whether the
clumps are coming from the stains or dPBS rather than the cell suspension
(added to plate map)

2) UPDATE: plain dPBS, Sytox green in dPBS, ReadyFlow Hoechst 33342 in dPBS
did not show contaminating debris BUT the pre-made stain solution did.
Therefore we can conclude that there was some problem in the preparation of
the stain solution.
