Process
  
  
  1. Took 2 x 50 uL samples from culture 
  2. Performed count using haemocytometer 
    1. Cell counts: 6,880,000 & 6,640,000 
    2. Average: 6,760,000
  3. Transferred 6 mL from culture containing 2M cells to 50 mL centrifuge tube
  4. Centrifuged at 500 x g for 5 min 
  5. Aspirated 100% of supernatant 
  6. Added 20 mL of 50/50 medium and re-suspended pellet 
  7. Strained cell suspension using 40 um strainer back into E125 flask
  8. Replaced E125 in incubator at 37'C, 5% CO2, 120 RPM
