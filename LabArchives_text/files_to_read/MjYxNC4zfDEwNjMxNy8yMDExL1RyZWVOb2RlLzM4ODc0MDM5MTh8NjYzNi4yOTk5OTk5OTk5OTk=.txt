Procedure


Prepared growth medium

  1. Pumped out 800 mL of growth medium and pumped in the remainder of the growth medium in the bottle to make up the difference to 1500 mL.
  2. Gathered materials to make 2.5 L of growth medium.
    1. Recommended: DMEM/F12 + 20% FBS + 1X P/S + Poloxamer 188 at 2 g/L + P38i
  3. Combined the growth medium ingredients into 1 x 2 L bottle and stored on the bench wrapped in aluminium foil.
  4. Programmed SUBS-A pump to remove 750 mL of waste every 48 hours for the next 6 days.
