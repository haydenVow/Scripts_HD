In House: Set 4 (2x10CS) & 5 (2x10CS) - 50% Feeds with VitC


Due to aggregation of cells occurring sooner than anticipated, 50% feeds
originally planned for 4th and 6th of August were skipped, and treatment
planned for the 9th August was conducted on 4th August instead.




Experiment Plan:

  

  




Procedure - 50% Feed (1 & 2)

  1. Turn on the water bath and set to 37'C.
  2. Aliquot required growth media into a sterile container/s and add Vitamin C (aka LAA2P) [stock concentration 100mg/mL] to a working concentration of 50ug/mL in growth media i.e. 500uL per 1L of media.
  3. Place in the water bath and heat for at least 20 minutes. 
  4. Transfer flasks to the BSC, wiping with 70% EtOH. Discard media from the flask. 
  5. Replace 50% of the media in selected flasks (Sets 4 and 5) with VitC treated media. Return to incubator and note the Flask ID.
