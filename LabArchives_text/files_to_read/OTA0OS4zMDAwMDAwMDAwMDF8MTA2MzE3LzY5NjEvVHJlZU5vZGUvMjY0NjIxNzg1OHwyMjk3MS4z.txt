

## Background

We have previously had trouble amplifying DNA regions targeted by guides for
number of our target genes. To date we have only had success in amplying CHOMP
DNA using the following primer pairs.  
  
P7 fwd + P8 rev - IGFBP4  
P13 fwd + P14 rev - AQP1  
  
We have had success amplifying wild type CHOMP DNA using primer pair P9-P10,
however the resulting PCR product was not a clean band, indicating that the
binding was non specific and cannot be relied upon for validating our CHOMP
mutations. As a result this primer pair was discarded.

We have since not been able to show successful amplification of wild type
CHOMP DNA for targets PTEN, PABPC1, RIPK1, ZIC1 or FASLG.  
Primer pairs that have been tested unsuccessfully include the following. (fwd
primer + rev primer)
