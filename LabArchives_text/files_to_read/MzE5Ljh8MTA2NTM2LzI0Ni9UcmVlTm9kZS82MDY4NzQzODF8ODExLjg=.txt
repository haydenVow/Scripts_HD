Experiment Plan:

  

  




Procedure - 50% Feed

  1. Turn on the water bath and set to 37'C.
  2. Aliquot required growth media into a sterile container/s and for Sets 1 & 3 add Vitamin C (aka LAA2P) [stock concentration 100mg/mL] to a working concentration of 50ug/mL in growth media i.e. 500uL per 1L of media. (Sets 2 & 4 DO NOT get treated with VitC)
  3. Place in the water bath and heat for at least 20 minutes. 
  4. Transfer flasks to the BSC, wiping with 70% EtOH. Discard media from the flask. 
  5. Replace 50% of the media in selected flasks (Sets 4 and 5) with VitC treated media. Return to incubator and note the Flask ID.
