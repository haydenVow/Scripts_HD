




Performed a 100% feed on clones in the 6 well plate of culture 13136.

Briefly

  1. Media was aspirated and discarded from each clone.
  2. Cells were washed with dPBS 
  3. dPBS was discarded and 3mL of growth media was added back to the wells (DMEM/F12 + 20% FBS + 1xP/S) 
  4. Cells were returned to incubate at 30 degrees 5% CO2.
