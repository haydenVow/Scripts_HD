Process:

Before staining, please check to see if cells are alive. Feel free to take a
few images

  

Fixing ( ~ 20 - 30 min)

  1.  Removed media from each well and wash with PBS.
  2. Washed twice with 200uL PBS 
  3.  Added  100 µL PFA for 15 min:  12:37 - 12:52 pm 
  4. Removed PFA (and add in PFA waste container) and wash wells with   200 µL PBS. 
  5. Wash with PBS 2 times 
  6. Add PBS, prarfilm and label with: Epic number + fix date + "spare plates for checking antibody staining' 
  7. Place in staining fridge
