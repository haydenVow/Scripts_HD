Process:  

  1. Aliquoted 90mL Nuvonis suspension media (without growth factors) into a urine jar
  2. Collected vessels from shaker, Centrifuged for 5 min at 500 RPM
  3. Aspirated and discarded 9mL media from each tube (leaving 1mL)
  4. Took 20ul samples to perform trypan blue staining and manual cell counts with a haemocytometer. See count spreadsheet here: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1DPzKubYxfHmWogVPihC-SSIBtIylyrBQE5kRX5Z8NtI/edit#gid=0.
  5. Discarded all cultures due to low counts, experiment terminated
