## Procedure  

  * Aliquot 270 mL of base medium
  * Add supplement ingredients and KSR to 270 mL aliquot as described in table below (this will result in ~300 mL aliquot)   
  

  * Split 300 mL aliquot into 6 x 45 mL aliquots and 1 x 30 mL aliquot




## Procedure

  * Add hydrolysates into each of the aliquots as described below:
    * Aliquot 1 Control (45 mL) - no hydrolysates added
    * Aliquot 2 Malt extract (30 mL) - add malt hydrolysate
    * Aliquot 3 Lupin extract (45 mL) - add lupin hydrolysate
    * Aliquot 4 Pea extract (45 mL) - add pea hydrolysate
    * Aliquot 5 Wheat extract (45 mL) - add wheat hydrolysate
    * Aliquot 6 Soy extract (45 mL) - add soy hydrolysate 
    * Aliquot 7 Soy/Wheat (45 mL) - add soy and wheat hydrolysate   


  * *One of the aliquots will contain two hydrolysate (wheat & soy) supplements, i.e., aliquot 7
