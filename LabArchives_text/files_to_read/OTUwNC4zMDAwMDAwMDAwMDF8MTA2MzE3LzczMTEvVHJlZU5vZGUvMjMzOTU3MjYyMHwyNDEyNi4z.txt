# Procedure \- Media top up

1\. Extract required growth media into aliquots (40 mL of each condition) and
place into a heated 37'C water bath for 20 minutes.

2\. Add required volume of growth media to each flask (20 mL). ``

3\. Transfer and incubate at 37'C on shaker plate at the recommended RPM.
