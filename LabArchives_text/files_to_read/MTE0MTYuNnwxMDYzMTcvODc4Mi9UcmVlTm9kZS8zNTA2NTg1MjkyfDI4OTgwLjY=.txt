

Cultures 11410, 11485-2, 11412-1, 11412-2, 11485-1 11488-2, 11491-2 and
11491-1 were moved to 6 well plates, out of the 120rpm shaker tubes.

Briefly

  1. Cells in shaker tubes were centrifuged at 500rpm for 5 minutes
  2. Media was aspirated and discarded
  3. Cultures were resuspended in 3mL of DMEM/F12+20% FBS + 1xP/S growth media then moved to single wells on an ultra low attachment 6 well plate. 
  4. Plates were returned to shake at 80 rpm.
